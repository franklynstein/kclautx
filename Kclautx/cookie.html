Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how KclautX uses cookies and similar technologies when users access or interact with our website, mobile applications, and related services.

This Policy should be read together with the KclautX Privacy Policy and any other applicable data protection notices published by KclautX.

KclautX processes cookie-related data in accordance with applicable privacy and data protection obligations, including the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on a user’s device when visiting a website or using an application.

Cookies help improve:

  • platform functionality
  • security
  • user experience
  • analytics
  • operational performance
  • and fraud prevention capabilities

Cookies may be:

  • temporary session cookies, which expire once a session ends; or
  • persistent cookies, which remain on a device for a defined period

How KclautX Uses Cookies

KclautX may use cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

Essential & Authentication Cookies

These cookies are necessary for platform functionality, security, and authentication.

They may be used to:

  • maintain secure login sessions
  • authenticate users
  • prevent unauthorized access
  • support account security
  • remember session preferences
  • and enable core platform functionality

Without these cookies, certain platform features may not function properly.

Essential, authentication, and security-related cookies may be processed on the basis of legitimate operational and security interests necessary for the proper functioning of the platform.

Analytics & Performance Cookies

These cookies help KclautX understand how users interact with the platform and improve overall service performance.

They may collect information such as:

  • pages visited
  • session duration
  • device information
  • browser type
  • traffic sources
  • user interaction patterns
  • and error diagnostics

Analytics data helps improve:

  • usability
  • security
  • operational efficiency
  • and service reliability

Analytics and other non-essential cookies shall only be activated where users provide valid consent.

Functional Cookies

Functional cookies help personalize user experience and improve convenience.

These cookies may remember:

  • language preferences
  • region settings
  • display preferences
  • and user interface customizations

Functional cookies shall only be activated where users provide valid consent.

Security & Fraud Prevention Technologies

KclautX may use cookies and related technologies to:

  • detect suspicious activity
  • prevent fraud and abuse
  • identify unusual login behavior
  • protect against unauthorized access
  • maintain platform integrity
  • and support AML, compliance, and security obligations

These technologies help support platform security, fraud prevention, and compliance monitoring activities.

Third-Party Services

KclautX may utilize trusted third-party service providers or processors for:

  • website analytics and performance monitoring
  • authentication and account security
  • fraud prevention and risk monitoring
  • infrastructure and technical support services
  • and platform functionality optimization

These providers may use cookies or similar technologies necessary for their respective services and may process limited technical, device, or usage-related information subject to applicable confidentiality, security, privacy, and data protection obligations.

Third-party providers may also maintain independent privacy or cookie policies governing their services.

NDPA 2023 Compliance & Consent

KclautX processes personal data collected through cookies and similar technologies in accordance with the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023 and applicable data protection obligations.

KclautX shall not rely on implied consent for analytics, functional, personalization, or other non-essential cookies.

Users may:

  • accept or reject non-essential cookies
  • manage cookie preferences by category
  • withdraw previously granted consent
  • and modify cookie preferences at any time through available cookie settings

Cookie Consent Banner & Preference Management

On first visit to the website or first launch of the mobile application, users shall be presented with a cookie consent notice or equivalent consent management interface allowing users to:

  • accept all cookies
  • reject non-essential cookies
  • or customize cookie preferences by category

Non-essential cookies shall not be activated prior to obtaining valid user consent.

Users may subsequently update or withdraw their preferences through available cookie settings or privacy controls.

Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow users to:

  • view cookies
  • delete cookies
  • block cookies
  • or restrict certain cookie categories

Users may adjust browser or device settings to manage cookie preferences.

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect platform functionality or limit access to some services.
