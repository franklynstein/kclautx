Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with KclautX?
Download the KclautX app from the Play Store or App Store, sign up with your phone number and email, complete your KYC verification, and you're ready to start trading crypto, selling giftcards, and paying bills.
What are the KYC levels and their limits?
Tier 1 unlocks ₦200K daily limits with phone + email. Tier 2 raises that to ₦2M with a valid government ID. Tier 3 removes daily limits entirely after a proof of address review.
How long do withdrawals take?
Naira bank withdrawals settle in under 2 minutes via NIP. Crypto withdrawals are processed instantly and typically confirm on-chain within 5–15 minutes depending on the network.
What cryptocurrencies are supported?
Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Solana (SOL), USDT (TRC20/ERC20), USDC, BNB, and a curated list of altcoins. We add new assets regularly based on community demand.
Are there any fees?
Naira withdrawals to local banks are free. Crypto trades use a transparent spread shown before you confirm. No hidden charges. Network gas fees apply for on-chain crypto transfers.
Is my money safe on KclautX?
Yes. We use bank-level encryption, store the majority of crypto in cold wallets, require 2FA on every withdrawal, and operate under Nigerian financial regulations with full KYC/AML compliance.
