Account Restriction & Suspension Policy

Effective Date: 15 June 2026  |  Last Updated: 15 June 2026  |  Version: 1.0

Introduction

KclautX is committed to maintaining a secure, compliant, and trustworthy platform environment for all users.

To protect users, platform integrity, operational security, and compliance obligations, KclautX reserves the right to restrict, suspend, review, or terminate accounts and transactions where necessary.

This Policy explains circumstances under which account restrictions, suspensions, or compliance reviews may occur.

Identity Verification & KYC Restrictions

KclautX may restrict or suspend account functionality where:

  • identity verification requirements are incomplete
  • submitted KYC information cannot be verified
  • inconsistencies are identified in user documentation
  • fraudulent or altered documents are suspected
  • required compliance information is not provided
  • or additional verification is required.

Certain services, transaction limits, or platform features may remain unavailable until verification requirements are successfully completed.

Suspicious Transaction & Compliance Restrictions

KclautX may restrict accounts, transactions, wallets, or platform functionality where suspicious, unusual, fraudulent, or potentially prohibited activity is identified.

This may include:

  • unusual transaction patterns
  • rapid movement of funds
  • structuring or threshold avoidance behavior
  • suspicious wallet interactions
  • sanctions exposure
  • fraud-related indicators
  • account misuse
  • suspicious crypto activity
  • high-risk transactional behavior
  • or activities inconsistent with expected account usage.

Transactions or funds associated with ongoing compliance reviews may be temporarily held, delayed, restricted, or escalated pending investigation.

Fraud, Security & Unauthorized Access

KclautX may temporarily suspend or restrict accounts where necessary to:

  • prevent unauthorized access
  • investigate suspected fraud
  • protect compromised accounts
  • mitigate cybersecurity threats
  • investigate suspicious login activity
  • or prevent abuse of platform services.

Users may be required to complete additional verification or security review procedures before restrictions are lifted.

Regulatory & Legal Restrictions

KclautX may impose restrictions or suspensions where required to:

  • comply with applicable laws or regulations
  • satisfy legal, regulatory, or compliance obligations
  • respond to law enforcement requests
  • comply with sanctions requirements
  • cooperate with financial institutions or payment partners
  • or mitigate legal or regulatory risk.

Platform Abuse & Prohibited Conduct

Accounts may be restricted, suspended, or terminated where users engage in:

  • abusive or fraudulent conduct
  • platform manipulation
  • identity misuse
  • use of stolen payment methods
  • sanctions evasion
  • money laundering
  • terrorist financing
  • account sharing or unauthorized third-party usage
  • illegal activity
  • or violations of KclautX Terms of Service or platform policies.

Notification of Restrictions

Where operationally, legally, and security-wise permissible, KclautX may notify users of account restrictions, suspensions, or compliance reviews through:

  • in-app notifications
  • registered email communications
  • or other available communication channels.

Users subject to account restrictions may also be directed to contact:

support@kclautx.com

KclautX reserves the right to withhold certain details where disclosure may:

  • compromise an ongoing investigation
  • violate legal obligations
  • interfere with regulatory reporting obligations
  • or create security or fraud risks.

Reasons for Restriction

Where legally permissible and where doing so would not compromise an ongoing investigation, security review, regulatory obligation, or compliance process, KclautX may provide users with the reason for an account restriction or suspension.

Certain restrictions relating to suspicious activity monitoring, fraud investigations, sanctions reviews, or regulatory reporting obligations may limit the information KclautX is permitted to disclose.

Effect of Restriction or Suspension

During a restriction or suspension:

  • users may continue to view account balances and transaction history
  • users may be unable to perform transactions, withdrawals, transfers, swaps, or other account activities
  • certain platform features may remain partially or fully inaccessible
  • and transactions or funds may remain temporarily restricted pending review.

Restrictions may apply to:

  • specific transactions
  • certain platform features
  • particular assets
  • or the entire account depending on the nature of the review.

Review & Investigation Process

KclautX conducts risk-based reviews and investigations relating to restricted accounts or transactions.

Review timelines may vary depending on:

  • the complexity of the matter
  • regulatory obligations
  • transaction history
  • supporting documentation
  • third-party dependencies
  • or the nature of the suspected activity.

KclautX aims to respond to user appeals or compliance review submissions within five (5) business days of receipt, although complex reviews may require additional time.

Users may be required to provide:

  • additional documentation
  • source of funds information
  • transaction explanations
  • proof of ownership
  • or other compliance-related information during the review process.

Appeals Process

Users who believe their account was incorrectly restricted or suspended may contact:

support@kclautx.com

Appeals or review requests should include:

  • the user’s registered email address
  • relevant transaction information
  • supporting documentation where applicable
  • and a detailed explanation of the issue.

Submission of an appeal does not guarantee restoration of account access or reversal of compliance actions.

Permanent Suspension & Termination

KclautX reserves the right to permanently suspend or terminate accounts where:

  • prohibited activity is confirmed
  • fraudulent conduct is identified
  • compliance risks remain unresolved
  • regulatory concerns arise
  • or platform abuse is established.

Where legally and operationally permissible, remaining eligible balances may be returned to the user’s verified bank account following completion of applicable reviews and deductions relating to chargebacks, disputes, liabilities, or compliance obligations.

Funds connected to confirmed fraud, sanctions exposure, money laundering, terrorist financing, or other prohibited activity may be frozen, forfeited, escalated to regulators or law enforcement, or otherwise withheld in accordance with applicable law and compliance obligations.

Limitation of Liability

KclautX shall not be liable for losses, delays, inability to access services, missed opportunities, transaction interruptions, or damages arising from:

  • account restrictions
  • compliance reviews
  • fraud prevention measures
  • transaction delays
  • regulatory obligations
  • or security-related actions taken in good faith.

Policy Updates

KclautX reserves the right to modify or update this Policy at any time to reflect operational, legal, regulatory, technological, or business changes.

Updated versions become effective upon publication on the platform.

Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
